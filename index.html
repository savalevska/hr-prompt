Prompt Patterns

Chain-of-Thought

Ask the AI to reason step by step before giving a final answer.

Think through this step by step:
1. First, consider the employee's tenure and role.
2. Then, evaluate the performance data.
3. Next, compare against the team benchmarks.
4. Finally, provide your recommendation with reasoning.

Few-Shot Examples

Provide examples of the desired input/output format.

Here are examples of good feedback:
Input: "Late to meetings"
Output: "I've noticed you've arrived after the scheduled start time in 3 of our last 5 team meetings. Punctuality helps us respect everyone's time."

Input: "Great presentation"
Output: "Your Q3 results presentation was excellent — the data visualisation made complex metrics accessible, and stakeholders commented on the clarity."

Now write feedback for: [your situation]

Structured Output

Tell the AI exactly what format and sections you want.

Provide your response in this exact format:

## Summary (2-3 sentences)
## Key Strengths (3 bullet points)
## Areas for Development (3 bullet points)
## Recommended Actions (numbered list)
## Timeline (table with columns: Action, Owner, Due Date)
